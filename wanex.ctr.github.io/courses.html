Courses Offered

Programming Course

This 12-week course covers the fundamentals of programming and software development using popular technologies such as Java and Python. You will learn how to write, test, and debug code, as well as work collaboratively on projects with other students. Upon completing this course, you will have a solid foundation in programming and be ready to take on more advanced topics.

Cyber Security Course

In this 10-week course, you will learn essential concepts and best practices related to cybersecurity. You will explore various security frameworks, tools, and techniques to mitigate risks and threats associated with modern network and web-based systems. Upon completion of this course, you will have a clear understanding of how to secure online systems and ensure their integrity and availability.

Business Analysis Course

This 8-week course provides you with fundamental knowledge and skills in business analysis. You will learn how to define business needs, gather requirements, and analyze data to produce effective solutions. The course also covers key concepts such as stakeholder management, communication, and project management. Upon completion, you will be able to apply your skills to various industries and contexts.
